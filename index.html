Canada

Canada is a country that is in North America and is the second largest country in the world in terms of territorial extension, behind only Russia. It has rugged terrain and climates that vary from Temperate, further south, to Arctic, in the north The Canadian population today is just over 37.7 million inhabitants, who are concentrated in the southern portions of the territory.The country has high social and economic indicators, currently being one of the largest economies on the planet.

The discovery of the Canadian territories was made by the navigator John Cabot, from England, in 1497. Their colonization began with the French in 1554. Over the years there were many disputes over land between England, Canada and the thirteen colonies ( U.S).Due to these disputes, the Quebec Act was issued by the English, which allowed the French in Canada. Therefore, to this day we have reflections of disputes in today's society, such as the two official languages ​​(English and French) in the regions.
