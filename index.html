canada

Canada is a country that is in North America and is the second largest country in the world in terms of territorial extension, behind only Russia. It has rugged terrain and climates that vary from Temperate, further south, to Arctic, in the north The Canadian population today is just over 37.7 million inhabitants, who are concentrated in the southern portions of the territory.The country has high social and economic indicators, currently being one of the largest economies on the planet.
